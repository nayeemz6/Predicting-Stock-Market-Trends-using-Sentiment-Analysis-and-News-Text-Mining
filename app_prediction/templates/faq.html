Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the purpose of this website?

A: This website aims to predict stock prices using machine learning and offers sentiment analysis based on given headlines.

Q: How do I use this website?

A: Simply input the required data like open price, high price, low price, volume, and a headline to get a prediction and sentiment analysis.

Q: How accurate are the stock predictions?

A: The predictions are based on machine learning algorithms and are as accurate as the data they are trained on. However, they should not be used as financial advice.

Q: What data do I need to provide for stock prediction?

A: You need to provide the following data: open price, high price, low price, and volume.

Q: How do I interpret the charts?

A: The charts provide a visual representation of stock prediction and sentiment scores, making it easier to interpret the data.

Q: I received an error message. What should I do?

A: Please double-check the data you've entered. If the problem persists, contact support.
